Ingredients:
 - 1 cup softened butter
 - 1 cup sugar
 - 1 large egg
 - 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
 - 1⁄2 teaspoon almond extract (optional)
 - 2 cups flour

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350°F.

 2. Cream butter and sugar.

 3. Add egg, vanilla, and almond extract, if using, and beat well.

 4. Mix in flour.

 5. Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto parchment-lined cookie sheet and bake 10-12 minutes.
